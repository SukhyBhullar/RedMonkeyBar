About

Serving you the best cocktails & mocktails for any occasion!

  • So, you're planning an event.

    You want it to be different. You want it to be special. You want it to be remembered. We at RedMonkey Cocktail Bar will make that happen for you!

  • We always bring a touch of class

    From small birthday parties to large corporate events, weddings and festivals. RedMonkey Cocktail Bar provide a range of cocktail-based services at any location within the UK, at any time of year, with each event customised entirely to suit your needs. We do not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach, so we offer a variety of bars, set-ups and menus.

  • We will always provide you with premium service

    RedMonkey Cocktail Bar provides you with premium cocktails and mocktails (we like sober monkeys too!), made with fresh ingredients by our professional mixologists. Whether you have specific ideas, or would like help in creating personalised cocktails, we don't mind.. we are just determined to put a smile on your face and stimulate your tastebuds.


    A tip - request our signature RedMonkey Cocktail.. it's sure to make you go bananas!

  • Book now!

    So, if you like what you have read, or just simply want to have a chat with us, please don't hesitate to get in touch. Allowing RedMonkey Cocktail Bar to play a key part in your event will be the best decision you make!
