Services

Here at RedMonkey Cocktail Bar, we provide a range of services that are fit for any event. Be it weddings, birthdays, festivals, themed events (the list goes on!), we have just the thing.

RedMonkey Unlimited Cocktails

Our most popular service is the RedMonkey Unlimited Cocktails Bar Hire. We take on every aspect of the service from start to finish, and so it is completely hassle-free for the host. RedMonkey Cocktail Bar will provide the bar, staff, glassware, ingredients, and charm, for the duration of the booking. All drinks are unlimited, and we completely tailor the service to the host's requirements. For further information regarding this option, please see our "Packages" section.

RedMonkey Cash Bar

The alternative to this is the RedMonkey Cash Bar Hire, in which we provide your event and its guests with very reasonably priced drinks for your requested set period of time. Again, you do not need to worry about anything once you have booked us as we provide everything required to service the event.

RedMonkey Bar Hire

We also offer a RedMonkey Bar Hire option. This is for those that want to hire one of our bars for their event, without any additional services. It's simple - you hire the bar, and you are free to use it in any way you please.. just remember, no monkey business of course!

RedMonkey Beer Bar

RedMonkey Cocktail Bar are able provide a beer bar for all events. We offer a bottled beer service, including full set-up, double glass display fridges, staff, glassware and any brand of beer requested. Alternatively, we can provide a fully functioning bar dispensing unit complete with taps, cooler, gas, kegs, staff and glassware.

Corporate Hire

RedMonkey Cocktail Bar offer an outstanding Corporate Bar Hire service, more of which you can read about in the "Corporate Hire" section.

Hen Parties

A hugely popular option on a hen night, as it is the perfect way to kick off an evening out with the girls. You’ll get to create all your favourite cocktails, from mixing mojitos to making martinis – and more importantly, you’ll get to drink your creations. The fun interactive atmosphere, the flowing cocktails – you can see why we recommend this!. We can either come to you, or you come to a central location. Either way, it'll kick off your night in the right way!

We are happy to discuss any of our services with you, so please don't hesitate to get in touch with us!

RedMonkey Corporate Service

RedMonkey Cocktail Bar can provide the ultimate in cocktail service, and class, for your corporate function. A complete package, charged per head, can be adapted to any setting with a guarantee of the following:

  • A LED Bar (we do offer alternative bar types)

  • Semi-Island LED bar
